Why We Do It?

We discovered that both new and experience fashion designers, enthusiasts and tailors all had a problem with creating and getting patterns for clothes they want to make. This has managed to stifle their creativity.

To solve this problem was what drove us to create this platform, The change we will be making and the testimonies of our users is why we will keep doing it.

How do we work?

You simply visit our websites, go to our upload page and follow the instructions there on how to get pictures of clothes you need pattern drafts for. Make payment and work will be delivered within 3 - 5 working days. If you have no style to upload, you can explore our website for different style, and use any of the pattern drafts you find there or for a token have the physical draft delivered to your preferred location.
